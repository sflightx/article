Maya Block 2 lifted off at March 25, 2024 4:50AM PhST (20:50 UTC) for the launch of Flight 3 into space. This mission carries 8 OmegaSat-mini, a smaller version of our satellite.

No hardware will be recovered, but the booster is expected to execute a boostback burn and a landing burn.

This mission, counts as sFlightX's 4th launch attempt and our 3rd orbital launch of 2024.
