Maya Block 6 is set to launch the ObSat 1-1 Mission for @ZastenX's Planet Observation Satellite on January 22, 08:25 UTC, at Terra Aurora, Droo which will carry the Planet Observation Satellite to High Earth Orbit.

Obsat 1-1 or Planet Observation Satellite is a planet imaging satellite that will be tasked to watch and record data that will be sent back down to Earth to be studied by teams at ZastenX. This will be the first of many launches of these types of spacecraft that doses theses types of jobs.

MISSION COUNTDOWN

This mission will debut the first launch of the Block 6 variant of the Maya family of ockets, increasing the second stage delta velocity or capability to further carry payloads to deep space. The first stage's aerodynamic improvements will help increase the efficiency of the rocket to safely return to the landing site.
