Maya Block 3 launched the EarthDust Sample Return Mission on April 18, 10:30 UTC at Launch Complex 1A, Bataan Space Center, Philippines which carried scientific instruments to Tydos and bring back Space Dust beyond the Kuiper Belt to Earth.

The vehicle, Block 3 will debut its first flight, with reusability options, should the first stage be capable of RTLS.

Mission Objectives:

✓ Reach Tydos
✓ Fly-by to almost 100,000km above Tydos, and collect Space Dusts
✓ Return to Earth as one piece!

This mission, counts as sFlightX's 7th launch attempt and our 6th orbital launch of 2024.
