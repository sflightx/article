Maya Block 3 launched the OmegaLink 3-2 Mission on May 12, 06:30 UTC at Launch Complex 1A, Bataan Space Center, Philippines which carried 5 OmegaSat v5 to LEO.

OmegaSat v5 is the new compact sized satellite for OmegaLink, a less weight, size and a modern version.

The first stage vehicle was expended for this mission.

This mission flew the last Block 3 of Maya , as we developed the Maya Block 5 vehicle.
