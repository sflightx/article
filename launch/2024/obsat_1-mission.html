Maya Heavy is set to launch the ObSat-1 Mission for @ZastenX's Planet Observation Satellite on June 11, 19:00 UTC, at Launch Complex 1A, Bataan Space Center, Philippines which will carry the Planet Observation Satellite to Low Moon Orbit.

Obsat 1 or Planet Observation Satellite is a planet imaging satellite that will be tasked to watch and record data that will be sent back down to Earth to be studied by teams at ZastenX. This will be the first of many launches of these types of spacecraft that doses theses types of jobs. In the coming weeks, teams at Zasten Satellites will be making a junction satellite or "JSat" that will get a signal from ObSat-1 and relay it down to Earth in a high quality format including images in 4K!.

This mission is the first commercial launch of Maya Heavy, second commercial launch of Maya after @STARJNO's EarthDust Mission.

This mission, counts as sFlightX's 11th launch attempt and our 10th orbital launch of 2024.
