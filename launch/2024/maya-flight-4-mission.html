Maya Block 2 lifted off at April 03, 2024 8:30PM PhST (12:30 UTC) for the launch of Maya Flight 4 into space. This mission carries 1 OmegaSat-mini, a smaller version of our satellite.

No hardware will be recovered, but the booster is expected to execute a soft targeted splashdown ~30km downrange the launchpad.

This mission will also feature a no-sep mechanism if flight computers detect deviation to expected trajectory.

This 2nd-last inaugural flight of Maya verifies her capabilities for rapid reusability, before going to be fully operational.

This mission, counts as sFlightX's 5th launch attempt and our 4th orbital launch of 2024.
