Maya Block 6, sFlightX's Flagship rocket is set to launch on its debut flight. Featuring enormous upgrades from block 1 to Block 5, this mission is the most ambitious yet in terms of rapid reuse and reliability. Liftoff is targeted on January 29 at Terra Aurora, Droo. Launch window opens at 3am Philippine Standard Time (19:00 UTC).

This mission, Maya will carry 5 Sky Shield Reaction Satellites from AlphaX Space's AlphaLink.
